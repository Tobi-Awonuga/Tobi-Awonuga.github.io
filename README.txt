Tobi Awonuga | Personal Portfolio

Overview

Welcome to my personal portfolio! This website serves as a digital showcase of my work, skills, and journey as a software developer. Designed with a focus on clean aesthetics, responsive design, and user-friendly navigation, this portfolio is a reflection of my passion for technology and my commitment to continuous learning and growth in the field.

Features
1. Dynamic Project Showcase
Interactive Thumbnails: Browse through my projects with interactive thumbnails that provide quick previews and easy access to detailed descriptions.
Project Modals: Each project is presented in a modal view with in-depth explanations, technologies used, and live demo links.

---

2. Responsive Design
Cross-Platform Compatibility: Fully optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, ensuring a seamless user experience across all platforms.
Modern Aesthetics: Clean, minimalistic design with a focus on readability and easy navigation.

---

3. About Me Section
Personalized Introduction: Learn more about my background, interests, and what drives my passion for software development.
Skills Overview: A detailed list of my technical skills, including programming languages, frameworks, and tools that I’m proficient in.

---

4. Contact Information
Integrated Contact Form: Easily reach out to me through a built-in contact form, or connect with me on LinkedIn and GitHub.
Social Media Links: Direct links to my professional social media profiles for further engagement.
---

Technologies Used
HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript: The backbone of my portfolio, providing structure, style, and interactivity.
Bootstrap: For responsive design and mobile-first development.
GitHub Pages: Hosting the portfolio, ensuring fast and reliable access.
Visual Studio Code: My go-to development environment for coding and debugging.

---

How to Explore
Home Page: Start at the home page to get an overview of who I am and what I do.
Projects: Navigate to the Projects section to explore detailed case studies of my work.
About Me: Learn more about my background, experience, and the skills I bring to the table.
Contact: Get in touch with me directly through the Contact page or connect via social media.

Installation & Setup
If you’re interested in setting up a similar portfolio for yourself:

Clone the Repository: git clone https://github.com/Tobi-Awonuga/Tobi-Awonuga.github.io
Navigate to the Project Directory: cd portfolio
Open in Browser: Simply open the index.html file in your preferred web browser.
